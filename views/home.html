What Is No Due Portal?

The No Due portal is a web-based platform that simplifies the "No Due" approval process for students and department authorities in a university. Students can create an account and request "No Due" approvals from various departments by filling out online forms and uploading necessary documents. Department authorities can review the requests and add comments indicating necessary actions for approval. Once a student's request is approved by all relevant departments, they can download a PDF of the approval. The system automates the approval process, reducing the time and effort required, and allows for easy access and tracking of requests. The No Due portal is a valuable tool that improves the experience of students and department authorities in the university.
